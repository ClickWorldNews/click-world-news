Pricing in plain English

  • Step 1: one-time build fee to create and launch your custom agent
  • Step 2: monthly ops fee to cover model/API usage, reliability, and ongoing updates
  • Step 3: if your usage is high, you move to a higher tier (so delivery stays profitable)

This structure prevents underpricing and protects quality as message volume grows.

Pricing + scope notice

  • Build fees cover agreed scope and launch deliverables.
  • Monthly plans cover maintenance inside the selected usage tier.
  • Heavy usage/new integrations/new workflows may require a plan upgrade or separate quote.
  • Third-party API/platform costs are client-billed or passed through at cost.

Response windows

  • Starter Plan: within 48 hours
  • Growth Plan: within 24 hours
  • Enterprise: custom SLA
